How to Use

  1. 1 Allow camera access when the browser asks
  2. 2 Hold both hands up, palms facing the camera
  3. 3 Touch thumb tip to index tip on each hand — a cyan frame appears
  4. 4 Bring both hands together and hold ~0.6 s to cycle to the next effect
🤏 Pinch gesture cycles effects.
The frame fills white as it counts down.
